Conclusion

So ... what does it all mean?

The Overgeneralization Hypothesis illuminates exactly what we are doing & why we do it, even if our judgments are mistaken
The outcomes of these impressions may be predictable, but they are subjectively based upon stereotype. Regardless of how inaccurate, they exert powerful effects.
There is a combination of cause & effect going on here, one in which perception influences behavior, but also in that behavior influences perception
