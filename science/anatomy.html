Anatomy

  • Chin
  • Forehead
  • Mouth
  • Eyes
  • Nose

Mature Chin: Strong pronounced cheekbones and a long, angular jaw

Baby Chin: Non-prominent, short, soft chin and less developed jaw

Mature Forehead: Short, receding in profile, with features higher on the plane of the face

Baby Forehead: Large, protruding profile, with features low on the plane of the face

Mature Mouth: Thin, small and straight lips, with the upper lip slimmer than the bottom

Baby Mouth: Full, heart-shaped lips, large in proportion to the rest of the face

Mature Eyes: Small, often dark eyes with a straight lid and strong brow

Baby Eyes: Round, large, wide-set and often light eyes

Mature Nose: Long narrow nose, usually full or downturned in profile

Baby Nose: Upturned or “button” nose, wide at the bottom and mostly concave in profile
